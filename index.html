DevConf 2026 runs October 14–16 at the Bayview Exhibition Center in San Francisco, a short walk from BART and Caltrain.

$ refund --policy + –

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, tickets can be transferred to a colleague at no charge.

$ sessions --recorded + –

Yes, all main-stage talks are recorded. Standard passes include 30 days of digital access, Pro passes get lifetime access.

$ discounts --student --group + –

Students save 50% with a valid .edu email, and groups of 5 or more save 20% per ticket. Reach out to the team to apply either discount.

$ checklist --bring-onsite + –

Bring a valid photo ID and your ticket QR code. Laptops are recommended for workshops; badges and swag are handed out at check-in.
